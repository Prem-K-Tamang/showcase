About Us

Promoted by the team of Education Professionals and Film Industry Professionals. The NFA is a unique and dynamic organization created to locate, educate, develop and promote the talents of exceptionally gifted upcoming young actors and cinematographers in Nepal today. It is the first and only ISO 9001:2015 Certified Film Academy in Nepal.
